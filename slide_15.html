Narration Transcript

Let's bring it all together.

Everything in this module connects. The Mission tells us why we exist. The Vision sets the standard we're aiming for. The I CARE Values define how we show up for patients, families, and each other. The Leadership Behaviors Model gives us a shared language for what good looks like at every level. The Focal Point Review and our development tools make sure that growth doesn't get left to chance. And all of it — every behavior, every conversation, every choice — produces the culture outcomes that make Houston Methodist what it is.

You aren't a passenger in Houston Methodist's culture. You are the culture. Every single day.

I CARE isn't a poster on the wall or the card you wear with your badge. It's how you treat your coworker in the hallway, the patient in the waiting room, the email at the end of a long day. It's lived — or it's nothing.

So before you close this out, two things you can do this week. First, go into Workday and request feedback from two or three colleagues — and ask people across roles, not just the folks you sit next to. Different perspectives reveal different strengths. Second, update your Career Profile in Workday. Fifteen minutes there makes you visible to leaders across Houston Methodist for opportunities you'd otherwise miss. These two small acts are what turn everything in this module into something real.
