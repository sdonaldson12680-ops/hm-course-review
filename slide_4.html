The value

"We hold ourselves accountable for our actions."

In your work

You made a mistake that affected a patient. You own it directly, take corrective action, and share the learning with your team. Or you're in food services and a tray goes to the wrong room — you don't wait for someone else to fix it. You own it, correct it, and close the loop. Accountability means the buck stops with you, no matter your role.

The value

In your work
