Slide 11 · Talent Management

Your growth is
not left to chance.

The Talent Management team exists to make sure HM has the right people, in the right roles, growing in the right direction — at every level. Here's how they do it.

Why this matters to you

These aren't HR processes. They're the system HM uses to make sure your contributions are seen, your potential is recognized, and your development is supported — continuously.

The Development Philosophy — A Continuous Cycle
Three Activities — One Continuous Cycle
1
Assess Potential
HM evaluates readiness for growth — not just current performance. Leaders identify employees who are ready for expanded responsibility, new challenges, or advancement. Your behaviors and engagement signal your potential.
2
Assess Performance
This is where the FPR lives. Your performance is assessed against agreed dimensions — including I CARE behaviors — through self-evaluation, manager evaluation, and a two-way development conversation.
3
Talent Review & Calibration
Leaders across HM meet to calibrate talent assessments and identify development opportunities. This ensures consistency, fairness, and that high-potential employees are seen — regardless of which campus or department they're in.
🔄

This is not a once-a-year event. These three activities work as a continuous cycle — your development is an ongoing conversation, not an annual checkbox.

Main narration (audio_11)

Now let's talk about the system HM has built to make sure your growth doesn't get left to chance.

The Talent Management team works behind the scenes to ensure that HM has the right people, in the right roles, growing in the right direction — at every level of the organization.

They do this through three interconnected activities — and together, these three things form a continuous cycle. Assess Potential — looking at who is ready for growth, new responsibilities, or advancement. Assess Performance — where the FPR lives, evaluating how you're showing up against agreed dimensions including your I CARE behaviors. And Talent Review and Calibration — where leaders across HM come together to ensure the process is fair and consistent, and that high-potential employees are seen regardless of their campus or department.

These are not three separate HR events. They are one continuous loop — and you are at the center of it.
