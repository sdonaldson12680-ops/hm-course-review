Slide 11 · Talent Management

Your growth is
not left to chance.

The Talent Management team exists to make sure Houston Methodist has the right people, in the right roles, growing in the right direction — at every level. Here's how they do it.

Why this matters to you

These aren't HR processes. They're the system Houston Methodist uses to make sure your contributions are seen, your potential is recognized, and your development is supported — continuously.

The Development Philosophy — A Continuous Cycle
Three Activities — One Continuous Cycle
1
Assess Potential
Houston Methodist evaluates readiness for growth — not just current performance. Leaders identify employees who are ready for expanded responsibility, new challenges, or advancement. Your behaviors and engagement signal your potential.
2
Assess Performance
This is where the FPR lives. Your performance is assessed against agreed dimensions — including I CARE behaviors — through self-evaluation, manager evaluation, and a two-way development conversation.
3
Talent Review & Calibration
Leaders across Houston Methodist meet to calibrate talent assessments and identify development opportunities. This ensures consistency, fairness, and that high-potential employees are seen — regardless of which campus or department they're in.
🔄

This is not a once-a-year event. These three activities work as a continuous cycle — your development is an ongoing conversation, not an annual checkbox.

One thing you can do today
Keep your Career Profile current in Workday.

Your Career Profile is how leaders see your skills, experiences, and development goals across Houston Methodist — even leaders outside your immediate team. When you keep it current, you're easier to find for stretch assignments, mentorships, and opportunities you might not hear about otherwise. Spending 15 minutes there is one of the highest-leverage things you can do for your own growth.
